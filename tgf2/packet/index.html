How to play

  Name Description
Objects Attack packets

If the player touched it, it will minus 10 from the player's health.

Spawns from the right with the interval specified on the top, e.g. "Delay: 1000ms".
It means it will spawns every 1 second (1000 milliseconds).

And it will focus/point to the player randomly with the chance specified on the top, e.g.
"Correction: 1/50 chances". It means it will focus/point to the player randomly with the
chance 1/50.


If the attack packet leaves you (punch to the wall), it will disappear and gives you "50 times Game Level" points.

Explosive

If the player touched it, it will spawn multiple "Attack packets" to all direction. And
make the "Attack packets" to focus/point to the player after 250 milliseconds.

Spawns from the right with the interval specified on the top, e.g. "Delay: 1000ms".
It means it will spawns every 1 second (1000 milliseconds).

Not similar to attack packets, explosive with not focus/point to the player randomly with the
chance specified on the top (Correction).

If the game level is 7 or higher, explosive will focus/point to the player randomly with the
chance 1/50. The chance will not be changed.

It will probably minus half of the player's health.
Super-explosive

Similar to "Explosive", it does the same thing.

But, if the player touched it, it will spawn multiple "Explosive" to all direction. And
make the "Explosive" to focus/point to the player after 250 milliseconds.

It will probably minus whole of the player's health.

-> Heal Packet:

Heals the player by adding 20 to the player's health.

-> Score Ball:

Adds "500 times Game Level" points to the player's score.
Game Control Mouse Simply use your mouse to control up and down.
Escape (ESC) Press escape (ESC) to end the game.

Hide debug field F4
